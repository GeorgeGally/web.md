What it does

A cleaner layer over the pages you already visit.

Extracts meaning

Readability and site-specific extractors keep titles, body text, links, comments, and metadata when they matter.

Removes ads and noise

Ads, navigation, cookie banners, sidebars, related-content blocks, and social widgets are stripped away.

Stays with you

Always-on mode follows your browsing session so links open in the same markdown reading layer.

Install locally. Browse quietly.

Build the extension, load `dist/` in Chrome, and turn ad-heavy pages into quiet semantic reading views whenever you need them.
